What is Timely?

Timely is a brand new academic time tracker! It is a peer to peer system where professors create courses, and add lessons and tasks for students to log their time throughout the semester. Students are able to keep track of their courses and log the hours they spend on specific coursework. Once time has been logged, Timely puts together the logged data and adds it into a chart where professors and faculty can see it all together!
